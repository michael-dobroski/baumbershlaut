How to Play Baumbershlaut

- 6-8 players gather in a circle like in the figure above

- The game will be played in single-elimination rounds

- Line chairs againt a wall, with the number of chairs being equivalent to the number of players minus one

- On the adjacent side, there will be some boundary, marked by a physical item i.e. broom or dry erase marker, the light blue line above represents where the boundary should be in accordance with the wall of chairs

- Before starting a round, someone taps the new category button at the top of this page to chose a category for that round

- Players go clockwise and when they step over the boundary, they must say something in that given category

- If someone passes the boundary without saying anything or repeats an answer, everyone screams BAUMBERSHLAUT!!!!!!!!

- This initiates the Baumbershlaut phase. Players must make it to a chair and sit in it. But, you can also try to grab the ball in the middle

- If any player is hit by the ball, they are out for that round. Otherwise, if no one is hit, the person who doesn't find a chair is out.

- If a player catches the ball, the person who threw it goes out instead

- Rounds repeat until there is one player remaining. They are hereby declared the Baumbershlaut Daddy and are looked down upon for the rest of their pathetic lives.
